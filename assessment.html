Mental Well-being Assessment

This self-assessment helps you reflect on your emotions, thoughts, and stress levels over the past two weeks.

The questions are inspired by well-known and validated mental health screening tools such as: PHQ-9 (Depression), GAD-7 (Anxiety), WHO-5 Well-Being Index, and the Perceived Stress Scale (PSS).

Answer Scale:
  • 0 — Not at all
  • 1 — Several days
  • 2 — More than half the days
  • 3 — Nearly every day

1. I feel little interest or pleasure in doing things.

2. I feel down, depressed, or hopeless.

3. I feel nervous, anxious, or on edge.

4. I worry too much about different things.

5. I have trouble relaxing.

6. I feel overwhelmed by daily responsibilities.

7. I have difficulty sleeping or resting properly.

8. I feel emotionally exhausted.

9. I feel confident in handling personal problems.

10. I feel calm and relaxed most of the time.

This assessment is for educational and self-awareness purposes only and does not replace professional mental health advice.
